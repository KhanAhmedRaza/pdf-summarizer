Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account allows us to save your summaries, track your usage, and provide a personalized experience. It also helps us prevent abuse of our service and ensure fair usage for all users. Your account enables you to access your past summaries from any device and add personal notes to organize your content better.

Free Plan (GPT-3.5): Provides concise, basic summaries with good accuracy for general content.

Starter Plan (GPT-4): Delivers more nuanced, comprehensive summaries with better understanding of complex topics, technical content, and specialized terminology.

Pro Plan (GPT-4 Enhanced): Offers the highest quality summaries with additional processing for maximum accuracy, better retention of critical details, and improved readability, plus access to bonus features like audio export and PDF chat.

Our fair usage policy ensures that all users receive consistent, high-quality service. While we provide generous monthly limits (50 PDFs for Starter, 200 for Pro), we may apply additional restrictions for unusually large documents or excessive usage patterns that impact our system performance. Unused summaries don't roll over to the next month. This policy helps us maintain optimal service quality while keeping our prices affordable.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately. When downgrading, your current plan will remain active until the end of your billing cycle, then switch to the new plan. For annual subscriptions, you can upgrade anytime, but downgrades will take effect at renewal.
